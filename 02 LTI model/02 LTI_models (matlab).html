Feedback connection¶

  • positive feedback $$G(s) = G_1(s)[I-G_2(s)G_1(s)]^{-1}$$
  • negative feedback $$G(s) = G_1(s)[I+G_2(s)G_1(s)]^{-1}$$
In [8]:
G1 = tf(1,[1,2,1]);
G2 = tf(1,[1,1]);

% negative feedback
G3 = feedback(G1,G2,-1)
G3 = zpk(G3)

% positive feedback
G4 = feedback(G1,G2,+1)
G4 = zpk(G4)

3.5. Similarity Transformation of State Space Model¶

ss2ss

\begin{align*} \dot{x}(t) & = Ax(t) + Bu(t)\\ y(t) & = Cx(t) + Du(t) \\ z & = Tx \\ \dot{z}(t) & = TAT^{-1}z(t) + TBu(t)\\ y(t) & = CT^{-1}z(t) + Du(t) \end{align*}
In [14]:
num = [1 7 24 24]; 
den = [1 10 35 50 24]; 
Gtf = tf(num,den);
Gss = ss(Gtf)

T = fliplr(eye(4)); 
Gss2 = ss2ss(Gss,T)

yy = [];

    [y,tout] = step(G,t);
    yy = [yy; y'];
